Case Studies> Boosting Bookkeeping Efficiency for a CPA & CFO Firm
The Customer

Our client was a well-established CPA firm offering high-end CFO and Tax services to mid-market businesses. The firm had built a strong reputation for strategic advisory work but faced growing capacity constraints within its bookkeeping function. Senior staff were increasingly absorbed in routine tasks rather than high-value advisory engagements.

The firm served a diverse client base requiring accurate, timely bookkeeping as a foundation for CFO advisory services. Internal bookkeepers were overwhelmed with reconciliation and data entry volumes. A reliable outsourcing partner was needed to handle routine operations while preserving quality and client confidentiality.

The Challenges

Routine bookkeeping demands consumed senior staff capacity, limiting the firm's ability to deliver high-value advisory services and accept new client engagements.

  • Senior staff overload: Bookkeepers and senior CPAs spent excessive time on routine reconciliation and data entry tasks.
  • Capacity constraints: Insufficient capacity prevented the firm from accepting new advisory clients despite strong market demand.
  • Revenue opportunity cost: Time spent on routine bookkeeping displaced higher-revenue CFO advisory and tax planning work.
  • Turnaround pressure: Client deliverable timelines were at risk due to bookkeeping backlogs and staffing limitations.
  • Software complexity: Managing multiple client environments across QuickBooks and Xero platforms strained internal resources.
  • Error exposure: Rushed processing under capacity pressure increased the risk of errors in client financial statements.
The Solutions

A dedicated bookkeeping team familiar with US GAAP standards was deployed to handle routine reconciliations, payroll entries, and financial statement preparation across the firm's entire client base.

  • Dedicated bookkeeping team: A specialized team was assigned to handle all routine bookkeeping across the firm's client portfolio.
  • US GAAP alignment: All work was performed in strict accordance with US GAAP standards and the firm's internal quality protocols.
  • Multi-platform expertise: The team operated across both QuickBooks and Xero, maintaining consistent workflows across all client environments.
  • Payroll entry management: Payroll journal entries were prepared accurately and on schedule across all client accounts.
  • Financial statement preparation: Monthly and quarterly financial statements were delivered with verified accuracy within agreed timelines.
  • Quality assurance: A structured QA process ensured all deliverables met the firm's accuracy and presentation standards.
The Result

The engagement freed senior staff capacity, accelerated deliverable timelines, and enabled the firm to expand its advisory client base significantly.

  • Senior CPA time freed: Senior CPA time freed by 30 hours per week, redirected to high-value advisory and client development activities.
  • Bookkeeping turnaround: Bookkeeping turnaround cut by 45%, ensuring timely delivery across all client financial deliverables.
  • Client capacity: Client onboarding capacity increased by 40%, directly supporting firm revenue growth.
  • Error rate reduction: Error rate in financial statements reduced by 90%, strengthening client confidence and firm reputation.
The Summary

The engagement relieved capacity pressure on senior CPA staff by deploying a reliable, GAAP-aligned bookkeeping team. Turnaround times improved, error rates declined, and the firm gained the capacity to onboard additional advisory clients. The partnership supported both operational quality and business growth objectives simultaneously.

  • 30 hours per week senior time freed
  • 45% faster bookkeeping turnaround
  • 40% more client onboarding capacity
  • 90% error rate reduction
