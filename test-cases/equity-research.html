The Customer

Our client was a financial advisory firm serving high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors. The firm required expanded equity research capability to identify counter-cyclical investment opportunities capable of protecting client portfolios during volatile market periods. Internal research coverage was insufficient for the analytical depth required.

The client needed a research partner capable of conducting rigorous fundamental and quantitative analysis across a broad universe of equities. Strategic recommendations needed to be grounded in verified data and aligned with risk parameters appropriate for the firm's diverse client portfolios.

The Challenges

Limited internal research capacity and insufficient equity coverage prevented the firm from identifying and capitalizing on counter-cyclical investment opportunities for its clients.

  • Insufficient research coverage: Internal coverage was limited to 50 equities, inadequate for the analytical breadth required.
  • No counter-cyclical framework: No robust framework existed for identifying equities likely to outperform in bear market conditions.
  • Quantitative analysis gaps: Internal teams lacked quantitative modeling resources required for rigorous equity screening.
  • Slow turnaround: Research turnaround times were too slow to capitalize on time-sensitive market opportunities.
  • Sector concentration: Research concentrated in narrow sectors created portfolio vulnerability for clients.
  • Data reliability: Inconsistent data sourcing undermined confidence in research conclusions and advisory recommendations.
The Solutions

Flatworld's research team conducted deep fundamental and quantitative analysis across multiple sectors, identifying defensive stocks, dividend aristocrats, and alternative assets suitable for challenging market conditions.

  • Expanded coverage: Research coverage expanded from 50 to over 200 equities across defensive and counter-cyclical sectors.
  • Fundamental analysis: Deep fundamental analysis was conducted on all covered equities using standardized financial modeling frameworks.
  • Quantitative screening: Screening models identified equities aligned to bear market outperformance.
  • Sector diversification: Coverage extended across multiple defensive sectors for well-rounded portfolio recommendations.
  • Alternative assets: Dividend aristocrats and defensive real assets were incorporated into research output.
  • Accelerated turnaround: Structured processes reduced time from analysis initiation to client-ready report delivery.
The Result

The engagement expanded coverage, improved analytical quality, and supported portfolio outperformance during a challenging market period.

  • Research coverage: Coverage expanded from 50 to over 200 equities, providing a broad analytical foundation.
  • Investment opportunities: 12 counter-cyclical investment opportunities identified and recommended to the client.
  • Portfolio performance: Client portfolios outperformed the benchmark by 8% during the bear market period.
  • Research turnaround: Turnaround time improved by 65%, enabling more timely advisory recommendations.
The Summary

The engagement provided the financial advisory firm with the research depth and analytical rigor required to build resilient, counter-cyclical investment strategies. Coverage expanded dramatically, turnaround improved, and client portfolios demonstrated meaningful outperformance.

  • 200+ equities covered
  • 12 counter-cyclical opportunities identified
  • 8% benchmark outperformance
  • 65% turnaround improvement
